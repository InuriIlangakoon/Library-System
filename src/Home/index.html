e-Library

In addition to print books, your local library is likely to stock a range of audio and e-books for a different reading experience. It is also likely to offer large print or braille materials.

Computer area

will often loan out CDs, DVDs and computer games, which can prove a much cheaper alternative to purchasing new ones.

Internet access

provide computers with internet access, enabling you to browse the web in a peaceful environment. If you have a portable devise you may also be able to access free WIFI.

Community clubs

library also often run clubs for parents, babies/toddlers and after-school activities for children.
